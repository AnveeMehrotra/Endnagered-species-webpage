Endangered Species


  • An endangered species is one that is highly likely to go extinct in the near future, either globally or within a certain political jurisdiction. Endangered species may be at risk due to factors such as habitat loss, poaching and invasive species. That's why we have to protect the animals. We need to stop poaching, hunting and illegal trading. All these things cause animals to die. If we keep on killing animals, then the eco system will get disturbed.


    • Now you must be wordering that if we keep on killing the animals then how will it affect the eco system.

    If we continue to slaughter animals, the ecology will be disrupted, and many creatures will starve as a result. For example, if animal 'A' eats animal 'B,' but animal 'B' goes extinct as a result of poaching and hunting, animal 'A' won't be able to eat anything else, and animal 'A' would die.
